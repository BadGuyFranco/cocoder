## Objective
The launch you use when you do not have a specific priority in mind. Oscar helps the founder either
(a) think through and draft a new priority, or (b) run a bounded, read-only support task against a
target repo, such as a code review or research pass on one of your repos, and report findings.
Verified when the session ends with either a drafted priority or a written review/research report.
Boundary: read-mostly; it never commits product code. Anything that should change code becomes its own
priority with its own Objective first; this session is the on-ramp to that discipline, not a way
around it.
