# Personas — Workspace-Custom

Workspace-specific persona overrides and additions. Distinct from the OSS persona contracts shipped in `../../packages/core/contracts/personas/` and the public playbook summaries in `../../personas/playbooks/` (created during Sub-Playbook B).

## Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `custom/` | Custom personas defined for this workspace only (e.g., a domain-specific extension via the Phil pattern) |

## CoCoder's own dogfood

For CoCoder itself, we expect `custom/` to remain empty in v0.1 — the six core personas (Oz, Oscar, Ian, Bob, Talia, Quinn) cover all our needs. The directory exists as documentation of the pattern.
